Pointwise Learning to Rank (Most common)

  • Predict absolute relevance score or class label for each item independently
  • Loss Functions:
    • Regression: MSE between predicted score and ground truth relevance
    • Classification: Binary cross-entropy or softmax cross-entropy
  • Training Tips:
    • Use click/impression logs; label clicked items as positive
    • Handle label imbalance with weighting or sampling
    • Suitable for CTR prediction, early stages of ranking pipeline
  • Examples: Logistic Regression, DeepFM, MLP Rankers

Listwise Learning to Rank

  • Goal: Optimize the entire ranked list at once based on list-level metrics (e.g., NDCG)
  • Loss Functions:
    • ListMLE Loss: Maximizes likelihood estimate (MLE) of correct ranking
    • ApproxNDCG, LambdaLoss: Directly optimize ranking metrics
  • Training Tips:
    • Input is a list of items with known relevance labels
    • Best for final-stage reranking
    • More complex but aligns closely with business objectives (e.g., NDCG@k)
  • Examples: ListNet, ListMLE
