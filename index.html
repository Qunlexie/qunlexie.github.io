Hi, I'm Dr. Olukunle O.

As a Machine Learning Engineer and Research Scientist with over 7 years of experience, I have led projects focused on building and deploying ML tools to solve complex problems. I specialize in applying ML to new, challenging, and difficult problems, with a strong emphasis on the practical application of ML.

My technical skills include a solid grasp of Python as a core coding language, and expertise in a wide range of modeling techniques, including Neural Networks, LLMs, Ensemble Models, Bayesian Optimization, Clustering Algorithms, A/B testing, Latent Feature Modeling, and Deep Reinforcement Learning.

Additionally, I am well-versed in Cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and GCP, as well as Big Data technologies such as Hive, Hadoop, and Kafka, and am proficient in ML Pipeline & Deployment tools like Airflow, Kubernetes, Terraform, and Dataswarm.

In summary, as an ML generalist with experience in both modeling and MLOps, I possess a valuable toolkit of technical and non-technical skills that make me an asset to any team or project.
